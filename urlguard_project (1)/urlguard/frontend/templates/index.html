What We Detect

Four Categories of URL Threats

URLGuard analyses each link against 33 structural, lexical, and heuristic features to classify it into one of four categories.

✓

Safe / Benign

URLs from legitimate websites with no indicators of malicious activity. Verified through entropy analysis, domain reputation, and structural checks.

google.comgithub.comwikipedia.org
🎣

Phishing

Fake websites impersonating legitimate services to steal credentials. Often use lookalike domains and brand keywords.

paypal-secure.tkapple-id-verify.pwamazon-login.xyz
⚡

Defacement

Websites whose content has been altered by attackers, often to spread propaganda or demonstrate a security breach.

Hacked home pagesContent injectionVandalism
☠

Malware

URLs that distribute malware — ransomware, trojans, spyware, or drive-by downloads.

exe-download.winfree-software.gqupdate-flash.cf

How URLGuard Works

A full ML pipeline from raw URL string to threat classification in milliseconds.

01

Feature Extraction
