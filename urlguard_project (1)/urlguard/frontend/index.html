What We Detect

Four Categories of URL Threats

URLGuard analyses each link against 33 structural, lexical, and heuristic features to classify it into one of four categories.

✓

Safe / Benign

URLs from legitimate websites with no indicators of malicious activity. Verified through entropy analysis, domain reputation, and structural checks.

google.com github.com wikipedia.org
🎣

Phishing

Fake websites impersonating legitimate services to steal credentials, passwords, or financial data. Often use lookalike domains and brand keywords.

paypal-secure.tk apple-id-verify.pw amazon-login.xyz
⚡

Defacement

Websites whose content has been altered by attackers, often to spread propaganda, embarrass organisations, or demonstrate a security breach.

Hacked home pages Content injection Vandalism
☠

Malware

URLs that distribute or facilitate malware — ransomware, trojans, spyware, or drive-by downloads. Identified through payload patterns and hosting characteristics.

exe-download.win free-software.gq update-flash.cf
